About Us

Working together to uplift the spirit of the Himalayas.

YOOO PAHAD FOUNDATION is a community-driven organization rooted in the heart of the hills. We bring together volunteers, local youth, and partners to create meaningful impact through social, cultural, and environmental initiatives — from organizing blood donation drives and cultural festivals to promoting sustainable tourism and environmental awareness.

Our Mission

Sustainability • Culture • Compassion

Our mission is to connect communities, preserve traditions, and empower lives in the Himalayan regions. We believe that every small step toward awareness, unity, and care creates a larger movement of hope. Through education, environmental conservation, and cultural promotion, we aim to build a stronger, self-reliant mountain society.
