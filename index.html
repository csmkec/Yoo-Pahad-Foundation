Our Story

Born from dreams, powered by youth, driven by love

Born from the dreams of Nainital's youth, Yooo Pahad Foundation is a vibrant movement transforming hillside communities through compassion and collaboration.

Officially registered on September 9, 2025, our journey began with personal donations and heartfelt commitment. Today, we stand as a beacon of harmony and hope in the hills of Uttarakhand.

We believe in blending fun with purpose, joy with impact. Every initiative we undertake is infused with the spirit of the mountains and the warmth of our people.
