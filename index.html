About Yooo Pahad Foundation

Yooo Pahad Foundation is a passionate non-profit organization dedicated to sustainable development, cultural preservation, and social empowerment in the Himalayan region.

Our mission is to ignite positive change in the hills of Uttarakhand by blending traditional wisdom with modern solutions. Through education, arts, and grassroots projects, we strive to create sustainable livelihoods, enrich cultural heritage, and empower our communities to thrive. Join us in celebrating our mountains and hearts‚Äîbecause together, we rise.

Our Focus Areas

We prioritize projects that uplift our community and environment

Education

Building schools and providing learning resources to ensure every child in the hills can access quality education.

Agriculture

Supporting organic farming, terraced gardens, and sustainable agriculture to feed communities while preserving the land.

Healthcare

Organizing health camps, providing medical supplies, and raising awareness for a healthier mountain population.

Art & Culture

Cultivating local talent by teaching traditional arts, dance, and music, and organizing cultural exchange programs.
